Slow Cooker Hawaiian Sticky Chicken

  • Time: 5 hours
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
skinless chicken breasts 2 pounds boneless
chicken broth 3 cups
pineapple juice 1 cup
crushed pineapple 1 cup
garlic 3 cloves
salt 1 teaspoon
apricot jam 1/2 cup
soy sauce 1/4 cup
teriyaki sauce 2 tablespoons
hoisin 1/4 cup
crushed red pepper flakes 1/4 teaspoon
Wondra® flour 1 tablespoon
hot cooked rice Add as much as wanted
scallions for garnish 4 chopped
sesame seeds for garnish Season to taste

Step 1: Place the chicken breasts, chicken broth, pineapple juice, crushed pineapple, garlic cloves and salt in your slow cooker and set on high.

Step 2: Cook for 4 hours then remove the chicken to a cutting board.

Step 3: Drain the broth from the slow cooker into a large measuring cup or bowl. Measure out 2 cups of the broth (including some of the crushed pineapple) and put back into the slow cooker.

Step 4: Add the apricot jam, soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, hoisin, crushed red pepper flakes and Wondra® flour to the broth and whisk to combine.

Step 5: Shred the chicken with two forks and return to the slow cooker with the sauce. Stir to combine the chicken with the sauce, cover and cook on high for 1 more hour.

Step 6: Serve over hot, cooked rice, and garnish with chopped scallions and sesame seeds.

Source: Recipegirl.com slow cooker hawaiin sticky chicken

Easy Grilled Chicken

  • Time: 20 Minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
chicken breast halves 4 skinless boneless
red bell pepper 1
zucchini 1
fat free Italian-style dressing 1 cup
green bell pepper 1

Step 1: Place washed chicken breasts in large sealable bag. Add 1 cup fat-free Italian dressing and close. Let marinate for 5 to 10 minutes.

Step 2: Cut up peppers into big chunks, and zucchini into big slices. Put into another sealable bag. Coat with leftover dressing.

Step 3: Grill chicken and veggies over medium heat.

Tip: Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.

Chinese Chicken Fried Rice

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
egg 1
water 1 tablespoon
butter 1 tablespoon
vegetable oil 1 tablespoon
onion 1 chopped
cooked white rice, cold 2 cups
soy sauce 2 tablespoons
ground black pepper 1 teaspoon
chopped chicken meat 1 cup cooked

Step 1: In a small bowl, beat egg with water. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium low heat. Add egg and leave flat for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from skillet and cut into shreds.

Step 2: Heat oil in same skillet; add onion and saute until soft. Then add rice, soy sauce, pepper and chicken. Stir fry together for about 5 minutes, then stir in egg. Serve hot.

Source: Allrecipes.com Chinese Chicken Fried Rice II
