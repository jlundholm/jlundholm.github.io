Below you will also find examples of student work - innovative game projects that demonstrate creativity, problem-solving, and technical proficiency.

My Courses

Three progressive courses designed to take students from fundamentals to advanced applications

IT 1

Information Technology 1

Foundational Level

An introductory course that establishes the fundamentals of IT concepts. Students learn about computer basics, file management, internet safety, and basic troubleshooting. This course builds a strong foundation for more advanced topics and prepares students for the digital world.

IT 2

Information Technology 2

Intermediate Level

An intermediate course that dives deeper into technology skills. Students explore productivity software, basic programming concepts, web development fundamentals, and collaborative tools. Emphasis on hands-on projects and real-world applications.

IT 3

Information Technology 3

Advanced Level

An advanced course focusing on specialized IT topics and project-based learning. Students work on complex projects, explore cybersecurity basics, database concepts, and prepare for industry certifications. Capstone projects demonstrate comprehensive skill development.
